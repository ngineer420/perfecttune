Tuner

← All tools

Play a single sustained note — a guitar string, a violin open string, a hummed pitch, anything — and perfecttune listens through your microphone, estimates the fundamental frequency with an autocorrelation pitch detector, and swings a brass needle to show how many cents sharp or flat you are against the nearest equal-tempered note.

Metronome

← All tools

A swinging brass pendulum keeps time the way a real metronome does, but the clicks underneath it are scheduled a fraction of a second ahead on the Web Audio clock rather than fired one at a time from a JavaScript timer — the standard lookahead-scheduler technique that keeps tempo sample-accurate even if the browser tab is busy or briefly throttled.
